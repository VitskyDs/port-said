PORT SA'ID

Port Sa'id is an ultra vibrant bar/restaurant in the heart of Tel-Aviv which celebrates vinyl culture alongside top-quality Mediterranean cuisine

A collaboration between celebrated chef Eyal Shani and Tel-Aviv's leading nightlife crew The Teder. We serve contemporary mediterranean dishes preparedfrom freshly-sourced local produce of the highest quality.


Whether you're a refined foodie or a music lover, you will not be able to resist our laid-back mediterranean vibes, shared-based plates, and rotating DJ's selecting from our extensive rare vinyl library.

We're located on a quiet pedestrian road overlooking the architecturally-acclaimed Tel-Aviv Synagogue, setting you with a year-round, good-vibes guarantee.
